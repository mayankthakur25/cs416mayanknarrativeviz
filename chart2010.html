The scatterplot above shows Total Enery Consumption in Mtoe on the y axis and CO2 Emission in Metric Ton on the x axis for different countries across continents in the year 2010. The color of the dots help us identify the continent that country belongs to. The top right region shows countries with the high energy consumption and high CO2 emission levels. You can hover over the dots to see the name of the country appearing in a rectangle just below the graph
